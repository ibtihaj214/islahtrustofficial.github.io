About Us

Khalid & Khalid Groups Of Companies

In 2011, Khalid and Khalid Group of Companies joined hands with the Akhuwat Foundation to establish ISLAH TRUST OF PAKISTAN, a remarkable endeavor dedicated to uplifting communities. With 13 branches and 3 area offices, ISLAH TRUST stands as an autonomous force for positive change. This trust boasts a robust infrastructure and abundant resources that enrich the lives of countless individuals. Its educational facilities offer knowledge and opportunities, while a well-equipped healthcare center ensures the well-being of the community. An orphanage provides a loving home for those in need, while a daily food distribution drive (langar) ensures that no one goes to bed hungry. Additionally, microfinance branches empower entrepreneurs, offering a path to financial independence and self-sufficiency. ISLAH TRUST OF PAKISTAN is a shining example of the incredible impact that collaboration and dedicated philanthropy can have on a society, creating a brighter and more hopeful future for all.

Our Aim

ISLAH TRUST was established with an aim to address economic disparity and growing illiteracy in Pakistan. The hallmark of its strategy is the alleviation of poverty through its Qarz-e-Hasna dispensation.

ISLAH TRUST - A Project of Khalid & Khalid Group of Companies

Khalid & Khalid Group has a firm belief in the concept that “progress is only progress if it uplifts the communities”. With its aim of upliftment of Pakitanis nationwide, K&K Group established Islah Trust - a social welfare arm of the group that provides affordable healthcare in rural and semi-urban areas, vocational training for youth in collaboration with the Akhuwaat Foundation, and disbursement of interest-free loans. Combined with a sense of social well-being, environmental sustainability, and nation-building, KK Group continues to empower multifaceted industrial sectors. The Khalid & Khalid Group has demonstrated its prowess in not only upholding the esteemed legacy of its Toyota Dealership but also in diversifying its portfolio to encompass a spectrum of industries, including ceramics, automotive assembly, industrial equipment, agriculture, social services and now successfully venturing into the realm of real estate. The legacy of Khalid & Khalid Group of Companies can be traced at : www.khalidandkhalidgroup.com/
