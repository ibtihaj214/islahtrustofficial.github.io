About Us

Khalid & Khalid Groups Of Companies

What originally commenced as a Toyota franchise has since undergone a transformative evolution into a multifaceted conglomerate. This diversified portfolio encompasses a wide spectrum of business ventures, spanning from the manufacturing of ceramic tiles to the importation of construction equipment, assembly of luxury buses and trucks, and, most recently, an entry into the real estate sector with the development of the region's tallest project.

INFLUENCING AND ENHANCING THE OVERALL WELL-BEING

In 2011, Khalid and Khalid Group of Companies, in partnership with the Akhuwat Foundation, established the ISLAH TRUST OF PAKISTAN, which is committed to diverse humanitarian endeavours, including the provision of educational facilities, healthcare services, orphanage , daily food distribution, and microfinance assistance and collectively contributing to the overall improvement of the quality of life for numerous individuals. The TRUST stands as a compelling testament to the profound impact that collaborative efforts and compassion can have on the lives of people. Islah Trust is headquartered in Rawalpindi, with its operations extending across the nation



Our Programmes

Empowerment Loan

Operating as the philanthropic arm of the Khalid and Khalid Group of Companies, this entity offers interest-free empowerment loans in rural and semi-urban regions. To date, the organization has disbursed over PKR 189 million towards social welfare initiatives, which include providing loans to support young individuals and distributing essential provisions in Ramzan to deserving families. With a network comprising of 13 branches and 3 area offices, this institution carries out its mission effectively.

LANGAR

Each day, we extend our food distribution program to reach around 1500 individuals across multiple locations within the twin cities. Our dedicated team ensures the preparation and distribution of top-tier, nutritious meals, all carefully overseen to maintain the highest quality standards.

ORPHANGE AND SCHOOLS

The school and orphanage stands as thriving institutions of education and personal advancement, where a wide range of school activities promote student growth and provide a fulfilling educational journey.

MENTAL HEALTH HOSPITAL

The mental health hospital caters to the underserved segment of society, offering comprehensive treatment for individuals with autism and various other mental health disorders by a team of skilled professionals. Beyond consultations, the hospital extends its commitment to the community by providing free medications as part of this initiative.

About Us

Khalid and Khalid Group of Companies laid the foundation of ISLAH TRUST OF PAKISTAN in 2011, in collaboration with Akhuwat Foundation. ISLAH TRUST operates as an autonomous community, with an aim to reduce the economic disparity and growing illiteracy in Pakistan.
