OUR PROGRAMS

Key Programs Of Our Trust

INFAQ

Provision of Interest Free Loan (Qarz-e-Hasna) that aims at economic well being and human capital development, with an average loan amount of Rs 15000 (approx).

EDUCATION

Establishment of educational and vocational Institutions. Islah Trust School empowers hundreds of students, fostering technical and non-technical skills.

TIJARAH

Fostering economic empowerment in marginalized communities via Musharaka and Modaraba principles, our focus is on small business support for a more inclusive society.

HEALTH CARE

Islah Trust Hospital guarantees premium medical care from qualified professionals. We extend free healthcare to those in need, emphasizing their well-being.

LANGAR

Serving 1500 people daily in the twin cities, we provide top-quality food prepared and distributed under strict supervision, ensuring access to nutritious meals.

ORPHANAGE

An orphanage with a family-like environment for abandoned and orphaned children where they receive love, care and emotional support along with formal education.

About Us

Khalid and Khalid Group of Companies laid the foundation of ISLAH TRUST OF PAKISTAN in 2011, in collaboration with Akhuwat Foundation. ISLAH TRUST operates as an autonomous community, with an aim to reduce the economic disparity and growing illiteracy in Pakistan.
